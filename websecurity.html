Web Security Overview

One thing I have learned from my computer classes is that the only way to 100% secure your computer is to never connect it to the internet. Since this is impossible for most people. There are a few things you can do to protect your computer and website from being vulnerable.

  • Make sure you have strong passwords and DON’T REUSE passwords. The best passwords for your websites/users/computer is those that you don’t even remember. To achieve such passwords you can download a password safe such as Password Keeper click here to read about this app.
  • Update often as people put out updates to protect vulnerable parts of their software.
  • Download Ad blockers- one recommended blocker is adblock+. This blocker will block websites that have adware. Only go into these cites if absolutely necessary.
  • Use encryption for everything you can.
  • Set up Firewalls! Firewalls! Firewalls!
  • Educate yourself and learn how to configure your computer.
